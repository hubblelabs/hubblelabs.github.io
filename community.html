community guidelines

Hubble is a platform that brings local communities closer together and helps building bonds with people who share the same interests. In order to achieve that, we've created these Community Guidelines to encourage a welcoming, creative and respectful environment for all of us. Below you will find some things, we as a community, won't tolerate.


adult content

  • We prohibit accounts that promote or distribute pornographic content.
  • Breastfeeding and other depictions of nudity in non-sexual contexts are permitted, however...

nudity or sexually suggestive content involving minors

  • Never post, save, or send nude or sexual content involving people under the age of 18 — even of yourself.

illegal content

  • Don’t use Hubble for any illegal shenanigans — including drugs, contraband, or illegal weapons.

invasions of privacy

  • Don't post someone's personal information or post links to personal information.

threats & violence

  • Never threaten to harm a person, a group of people, or someone’s property.
  • Don't post content glorifying violence.

harassment & bullying

  • We have zero tolerance for bullying or harassment of any kind on Hubble.

impersonation

  • Don’t pretend to be someone you’re not — including your friends, celebrities, brands, or other organizations.

hate speech

  • Don't post any content that demeans, defames, or promotes discrimination on the basis of race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, or veteran status.

terrorism

  • We have zero tolerance for content that promotes terrorism.

spam

  • We have zero tolerance for spam.
