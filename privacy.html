privacy policy

effective august 2, 2018

welcome to hubble!
Our mission is to bring local communities closer together. To achieve that all Hubble users share some information with us and with this policy we try to help you understand what information we collect, how we use it and what choices you have about it.


the two ways we collect information:
1.when you voluntarily provide it to us or give us permission to obtain it

When you sign up for and interact on Hubble, we collect the information you choose to give us voluntarily. The types of data we may collect include your username, email address, password, photos or any other useful identifying information. Being a locally focus platform we also ask for your permission to collect data about your location to provide local information on your surroundings as hubs, events or other relevant sites. Your choice, which hubs and spaces you join is fully optional, but we are required to process this information in order to administer your account, and to indicate that you are a member/user of the a hub or space that you join. To make it easier for you to invite others to join Hubble, we may also collect information from you device's phonebook, if you give us permission to.

Remember that you also provide us whatever information and content you send through our service, such as Posts, Comments or Messages.


2.technical information generated when you use Hubble

When you use Hubble or any other mobile application, some information gets created and logged automatically. Here are some types of information we collect:

  • log data.Every time you use Hubble, our servers record so called "log data" which is sent to us when you use our mobile app. This data includes, among other things, details about your activity on the platform, including time, duration and frequency of your usage; hubs and spaces you've visited and your IP address.
  • device information.We collect information about the mobile device you use to access Hubble, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information and crash data that helps us understand problems you may have when using Hubble.
  • location information.We may collect information about the location of your device each time you use our platform based on your consent to the collection of this information.


what we do with the information we collect:
We use the information we collect to operate and improve our service. In detail we use this data to,

  • develop, operate, improve, deliver, maintain, and protect our service.
  • communicate with you, including by email. For example, to respond to support inquiries or to share updates about our service and promotional offers that we think may interest you via email or push notifications, depending on your settings.
  • conduct analytics on how Hubble is being used by its users.
  • to protect the rights, property, or safety of Hubble and others; to enforce compliance with our Terms of Service and policies; and to comply with applicable law and government requests.
  • verify your identity and prevent fraud or other unauthorized or illegal activity.


how we share and disclose information:
We may share information about you in the following ways:

With other Hubble users & general public
  • information about you, such as your username, profile picture and hub memberships.
  • content you post or share in any way, depending on your personal, hub and space specific settings.
  • information about how you've interacted with our service such as your role(s) you may hold in a hub(s).

With third parties (if reasonably necessary)
  • to comply with any valid legal process, governmental request, or applicable law, rule, or regulation.
  • to investigate, remedy, or enforce potential Terms of Service violations.
  • to protect the rights, property, and safety of us, our users, or others.
  • to detect and resolve any fraud or security concerns.


transferring your information:
Hubble is a worldwide service, based in the United States. By using our products or services, you authorize us to transfer and store your information outside your home country, including in the United States, for the purposes described in this policy. The privacy protections and the rights of authorities to access your personal information in such countries may not be equivalent to those of your home country.

We as Hubble comply with the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and the Swiss – U.S. Privacy Shield Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information transferred from the European Union and Switzerland to the United States, respectively. Hubble has certified to the Department of Commerce that it adheres to the Privacy Shield Principles.


control your information:
We believe that control over your information is essential, so we provide you with the following choices:

  • edit information in your profile at any time.
  • change granted permissions, as location information or enabled notifications, by simply adjusting the settings on your device. Note that our service or its features may no longer function properly if you do.
  • access the information we hold about you. We'll usually share this with you within 30 days of you asking us for it.
  • delete your account at any time. When you close your account, we’ll deactivate it and remove all your data from Hubble. Nevertheless we may retain some personal data for certain legal, security, and business needs.


how long we keep your information:
We keep your information only so long as we need it to provide Hubble to you and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. When we no longer need to use your information and there is no need for us to keep it to comply with our legal or regulatory obligations, we’ll either remove it from our systems or depersonalize it so that we can't identify you.


children:
Children under 13 are not allowed to use Hubble. If you are based in the EEA you may only use Hubble if you are over the age at which you can provide consent to data processing under the laws of your country or if verifiable parental consent for your use of Hubble has been provided to us.


revisions to the privacy policy:
We may change this policy from time to time and if we do, we’ll post any changes on this page. If you continue to use Hubble after those changes are in effect, you agree to the new policy. If the changes are significant, we may provide a more prominent notice or get your consent, as required by law.
